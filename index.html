#laraveldk user group

This is the website of the #laraveldk user group, which at the moment isn't much more than a mailing list.

Our mailing list

You can (and you should) join our mailing list over at Google Groups. It's called #laraveldk. The purpose of the mailing list is to discuss various Laravel related topics, and also to make announcements about meetups etc. At the moment we are trying to arrange a mini conference / hackathon.

FAQ

1. Why are we speaking english
Because everyone are welcomed :)

2. Who can join
Everyone, regarding language or location, with an interest in Laravel, can join the mailing list.

3. How can I help
First of all, join the mailing list and participate in the conversation. We need input and ideas for upcoming events.
